What David Fixes

Services

If it's part of your RV, David can probably help. Here's what he works on most.

🚿

Dump Valve Replacement

Leaking or stuck dump valves are messy and frustrating. David replaces black and gray tank valves cleanly and completely, with zero shortcuts.

🌡️

Thermostat & HVAC

RV thermostats, AC units, and furnaces have their own quirks. David diagnoses and repairs heating and cooling systems so you're comfortable in any Texas weather.

💧

Plumbing & Water Systems

Leaks, low pressure, water pump issues, fresh or gray water tank problems — David handles all of it. He'll also explain what caused the problem so it doesn't happen again.

🔥

Water Heater Installation & Repair

Propane or electric, tank or tankless — David installs and repairs RV water heaters and gets hot water flowing again the right way.

⚡

Electrical Systems

Shore power hookups, 12V systems, lighting, inverters, battery banks, and more. David tracks down electrical gremlins and fixes them safely.

🏠

Roof & Seal Inspection

A small seam gap becomes a big water damage problem fast. David inspects roofs and reseals openings around vents, AC units, and trim before leaks get expensive.

🔩

General RV Maintenance

Slide-outs, awnings, leveling systems, propane checks, seasonal prep — if you're not sure what it needs, David can do a full look-over and give you a straight answer.

❓

Don't see your issue?

Give David a call. Chances are he's seen it before. If he can't fix it, he'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction.

About David

A neighbor you can trust with your home on wheels.

David Price isn't a franchise technician punching a clock. He's a local guy who genuinely loves working on RVs and takes pride in doing things right — the first time.

He's the kind of technician who will walk you through exactly what was wrong, show you the old part he replaced, and explain what to watch for going forward. No mystery charges. No repairs you didn't ask for. No pressure to upgrade something that doesn't need upgrading.

Some customers have been surprised when David walks them through a simple fix they can handle themselves — because he'd rather build a relationship built on trust than squeeze an extra hundred dollars out of a job that didn't need his hands. That's just how he works.

Located at 1183 S FM 1138 in Royse City, right between Fate and Nevada, David serves all the surrounding communities — from Rockwall County across to Collin County.
